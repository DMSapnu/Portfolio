Miguel Sapnu is a BS Forestry student from the University of the Philippines Los Banos. He is a former Internal Affairs Committee Head in the organization. Currently, he is into street and conceptual Photography. He wants to venture as many genre as possible. Versatility is his principle as a photographer. He believes anyone with passion for appreciating and creating photographs can be a photographer; creativity, passion, and patience is only the necessity.

Miguel Sapnu is a former Publicity Committee Head for the UP Munsci Alumni Society, a resident member of the UP Creo which trains college student in advertising and marketing . He is knowledgeable with Photoshop and Illustrator.

Miguel Sapnu's youtube channel consist of timelapse and astral photography. His plans for the channels include vlogs and photography equipment reviews.
